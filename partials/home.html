GMO food

Genetically Modified Organisms (GMOs) have been a topic of considerable debate and scientific exploration. These organisms have been altered through genetic engineering to exhibit traits not naturally found in their species, often with the aim of enhancing crop yield, resistance to pests, or nutritional content. Advocates of GMOs argue that they hold the potential to address food security challenges by producing more robust and nutritious crops, thus helping to feed a growing global population.
World Health Organization, "20 Questions on Genetically Modified Foods"
